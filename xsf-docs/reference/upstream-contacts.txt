Almost everything:

   link:http://wiki.x.org/wiki/Development/Documentation/SubmittingPatches[SubmittingPatches]

   link:http://cgit.freedesktop.org/xorg/doc/xorg-docs/tree/MAINTAINERS[MAINTAINERS]

 * VMMouse & VMWare: `thellstrom@vmware.com` — source:
